Assignment

Designing for unemployed workers working in the Australian Arts Sector to prepare for the future after a global pandemic

How can we create a structured schedule that offers access to improving skills so that art workers are better prepared to reenter the workforce and are less likely to become long-term unemployed?

My Role

Project Manager

Desk research, user research, ideation, wirerframing, testing

Timeline

4 Weeks

Methods

Desk Research, Interviews, Card Sorting, Lean UX Canvas, Empathy Mapping, Personas, Sitemap, Sketching, Prototyping, Testing

View

Write and design an interactive choice-based narrative adventure mobile game that simultaneously informs users about occupational violence and provides entertaining gameplay through unique storylines and characters.
